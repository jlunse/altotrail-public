# Product Overview

AltoTrail is a multilingual job-search and labour-market exploration product in
active development.

## Product goal

AltoTrail aims to help people understand work opportunities across languages by
connecting job discovery with occupation, skill, and labour-market context.

The product is intended to support users who want to:

- Explore jobs and related occupations.
- Understand how skills connect to work opportunities.
- Navigate public job-search information in multiple languages.
- Compare labour-market context without needing to understand internal data
  systems.

## Public product principles

AltoTrail is designed around a few public-facing principles:

- Multilingual access should be a core product feature, not an afterthought.
- Public pages should be useful to humans first and search engines second.
- User-specific features should be opt-in, controlled, and privacy-conscious.
- Data and generated content should be presented with appropriate caution.
- Operational and commercial internals should remain private.

## Commercial status

AltoTrail is independently developed and may be operated commercially, including
advertising, partnerships, and paid product development.

This repository is documentation-only and does not grant rights to reuse the
AltoTrail brand, private implementation, datasets, production systems, or
commercial internals.

## What is intentionally not included

This public repository does not include production code, deployment details,
operational runbooks, generated datasets, prompts, job alert internals, cache
implementation, credentials, tokens, private URLs, analytics identifiers, or
personal data.
